Healthcare is Broken

Costs skyrocket but healthspan and lifespan are decreasing

Rising Healthcare Costs

U.S. healthcare spending reached $4.5 trillion in 2024, representing 17.3% of GDP - more than any other developed nation. The average American now spends over $13,000 annually on healthcare...

U.S. healthcare spending reached $4.5 trillion in 2024, representing 17.3% of GDP - more than any other developed nation. The average American now spends over $13,000 annually on healthcare, with costs rising 4-6% per year. Despite this massive investment, Americans face mounting medical debt, with 100 million people struggling to pay medical bills. The system prioritizes reactive treatment over prevention, leading to skyrocketing costs for managing preventable chronic diseases.

Decreasing Life Expectancy

For the first time in a century, U.S. life expectancy has declined for three consecutive years, dropping to 76.4 years - the lowest since 1996. This places America behind 40+ other countries...

For the first time in a century, U.S. life expectancy has declined for three consecutive years, dropping to 76.4 years - the lowest since 1996. This places America behind 40+ other countries, including Cuba and Estonia. The causes are largely preventable: chronic inflammation-related diseases, metabolic disorders, and cardiovascular disease account for most premature deaths. Meanwhile, countries spending less on healthcare but investing more in prevention see their life expectancy continue to rise.

Reduced Healthspan

While total lifespan decreases, healthspan - the years lived in good health - is declining even faster. Americans now spend an average of 12-14 years living with chronic disease and disability before death...

While total lifespan decreases, healthspan - the years lived in good health - is declining even faster. Americans now spend an average of 12-14 years living with chronic disease and disability before death. Nearly 60% of adults have at least one chronic condition, and 42% have two or more. Inflammatory diseases like diabetes, heart disease, arthritis, and Alzheimer's rob people of quality life decades before death. The current healthcare system waits until disease is advanced rather than catching and preventing inflammation-driven disease early.

Why Inflammation

Understanding your body's inflammatory response provides critical insights into your health status, disease risk, and treatment effectiveness across multiple conditions.

IL-6 is a key inflammatory mediator that plays crucial roles in immune response, metabolism, and tissue repair processes.

Key Applications:

  • Depression and mood disorder monitoring
  • Cardiovascular disease risk assessment
  • Autoimmune disease activity tracking
  • Post-exercise recovery monitoring
  • Aging and inflammaging indicators
  • Cancer-related inflammation detection

TNF-α is a potent pro-inflammatory cytokine central to immune system activation and chronic inflammatory conditions.

Key Applications:

  • Rheumatoid arthritis and autoimmune monitoring
  • IBD and gastrointestinal inflammation
  • Metabolic syndrome and insulin resistance
  • Sepsis and acute infection detection
  • Anti-TNF therapy effectiveness monitoring
  • Neuroinflammation assessment

IL-1β is a master regulator of inflammation, driving fever, acute phase responses, and tissue damage in various diseases.

Key Applications:

  • Acute infection and fever monitoring
  • Gout and crystal arthropathy detection
  • Alzheimer's and neurodegeneration tracking
  • Post-surgical inflammation assessment
  • Autoinflammatory syndrome monitoring
  • Wound healing and tissue repair

IFN-γ is a critical immune activator that enhances pathogen clearance and regulates adaptive immune responses.

Key Applications:

  • Viral and intracellular infection monitoring
  • Tuberculosis and mycobacterial detection
  • Cancer immunotherapy response tracking
  • Autoimmune disease activity assessment
  • Vaccine response evaluation
  • Immunodeficiency screening

IL-10 is a powerful anti-inflammatory cytokine that regulates immune responses and promotes tissue healing.

Key Applications:

  • Treatment response monitoring
  • Immune system balance assessment
  • Post-infection recovery tracking
  • Chronic fatigue and burnout evaluation
  • Allergic reaction monitoring
  • Pregnancy and maternal health
