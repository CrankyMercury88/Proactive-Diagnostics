Why Inflammation

Understanding your body's inflammatory response provides critical insights into your health status, disease risk, and treatment effectiveness across multiple conditions.

IL-6 is a key inflammatory mediator that plays crucial roles in immune response, metabolism, and tissue repair processes.

Key Applications:

  • Depression and mood disorder monitoring
  • Cardiovascular disease risk assessment
  • Autoimmune disease activity tracking
  • Post-exercise recovery monitoring
  • Aging and inflammaging indicators
  • Cancer-related inflammation detection

TNF-α is a potent pro-inflammatory cytokine central to immune system activation and chronic inflammatory conditions.

Key Applications:

  • Rheumatoid arthritis and autoimmune monitoring
  • IBD and gastrointestinal inflammation
  • Metabolic syndrome and insulin resistance
  • Sepsis and acute infection detection
  • Anti-TNF therapy effectiveness monitoring
  • Neuroinflammation assessment

IL-1β is a master regulator of inflammation, driving fever, acute phase responses, and tissue damage in various diseases.

Key Applications:

  • Acute infection and fever monitoring
  • Gout and crystal arthropathy detection
  • Alzheimer's and neurodegeneration tracking
  • Post-surgical inflammation assessment
  • Autoinflammatory syndrome monitoring
  • Wound healing and tissue repair

IFN-γ is a critical immune activator that enhances pathogen clearance and regulates adaptive immune responses.

Key Applications:

  • Viral and intracellular infection monitoring
  • Tuberculosis and mycobacterial detection
  • Cancer immunotherapy response tracking
  • Autoimmune disease activity assessment
  • Vaccine response evaluation
  • Immunodeficiency screening

IL-10 is a powerful anti-inflammatory cytokine that regulates immune responses and promotes tissue healing.

Key Applications:

  • Treatment response monitoring
  • Immune system balance assessment
  • Post-infection recovery tracking
  • Chronic fatigue and burnout evaluation
  • Allergic reaction monitoring
  • Pregnancy and maternal health
